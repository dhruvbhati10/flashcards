# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 15, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how AI FlashCards ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data
We do not collect or store any personal data (such as your name, email address, or phone number) on our servers. All your flashcard data, decks, and study progress are stored locally on your device using Apple's CloudKit/SwiftData (if applicable) or local storage.

### 1.2 Usage Data
We may collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the App. This includes crash reports and performance metrics, which are collected via Apple's built-in analytics if you have opted in to share analytics with App Developers.

### 1.3 AI Generation Data
When you use the AI generation feature, the text or topics you provide are sent to a third-party AI provider (Google Gemini API) to generate the flashcard content. This data is processed solely for the purpose of generating the response and is not stored by us. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:
- Provide and maintain the functionality of the App.
- Generate flashcards based on your input.
- Improve the App's performance and user experience.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a central server database of user study materials.

## 4. In-App Purchases
All financial transactions are handled directly by Apple's App Store. We do not see or store your credit card information.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
